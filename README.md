
**Mangrove Biomass & Carbon Calculator**  
This project estimates species-specific mangrove biomass and carbon stocks from aboveground vegetation field data. It applies published allometric equations from the scientific literature to convert field measurements into biomass and carbon estimates. It also aggregates species-level data into plot or site level and generates comparison charts.

---

**Features**  
Supports multiple mangrove species  
Uses established allometric equations from peer-reviewed studies  
Calculates aboveground biomass and carbon stocks  
Aggregates species data to plot/site level  
Simple and adaptable to different field datasets  

---

**Input**  
Field measurements (e.g., DBH, height, species)

---

**Output**  
Species-level biomass estimates
Carbon stock calculations
